How well do you know your flowers?




Name the flower that has bright yellow petals, and blows away in the wind.

Dandelion.




Name the flower that releases a sweet smell and has layered petals.

Rose.




Name the flower that grows fire colored flowers and is most popularly known as "butterfly weed."

Milkweed.




Name the flower that has delicate white petals and a sunny yellow middle.

Daisy.




Name the flower that basks in the sun's glory.

Sunflower.
